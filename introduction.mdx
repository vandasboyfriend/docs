## What is Vision?
Vision is new-gen anti-detect solution for general purpose. You can use it for affiliate marketing or multi-accounting in social networks like FB, Twitter, Discord and others. But also you can use Vision for work with Google Ads, cryptocurrencies listing platforms, marketplaces and much more. 

## Why it's next-gen?
In creating our product we have emphasised on performance and stability and now we have maximum speed for all operations in Vision. You will not need to wait about 1 minute to make your profile synced with cloud. All processes are very fast and stable.
